Leadership

Owen Marr (Founder)

Petoskey, MI


Owen has been passionate about cars since he was a child. He founded NMSE in the Fall of 2017 with the simple goal of providing excellent service and an amazing experience to residents and visitors of Northern Michigan. Outside of NMSE, Owen works on aerodynamic packages for Formula SAE cars and on various space based projects through SEDS@UM. His primary focus and passion however is NMSE, and he works tirelessly to help ensure steady growth and improvement of the customer experience.

Kartikeya Kandula (Director of Technology)

Okemos, MI


Kart is in charge of all things technology at NMSE. A fellow University of Michigan student majoring in computer science, Kart runs NMSE’s website and all other technological facets of NMSE. He has had several internships at companies such as Delta Dental of Michigan and JPMorgan Chase, and his unique skill set allows NMSE to have a seamless and smooth rental process. Kart helps to ensure your experience NMSE is simple, worry free and secure.

Palmer Stemmler (Director of Finance)

St. Louis, MO


Palmer studies at the University of Michigan, majoring in economics. Palmer handles NMSE’s market analysis and strategy, helping NMSE effectively reach its customers and plan for the future. Outside of the classroom, Palmer is a member of the MFAMS financial group.

Ben Trask (Director of Operations)

Springfield, IL


Ben Trask studies economics at the University of Michigan. At NMSE, Ben focuses on the customer experience. He runs day to day operations alongside Owen to make sure each customer is able to have the adventure they desire. Outside of NMSE, Ben is the President of Theta Delta Chi at the University of Michigan.
